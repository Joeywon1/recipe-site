Simple/Sweet Peanut Butter Cookies

A three ingredient dessert perfect for a short notice sweet.

Ingredients

  • 200 g Peanut Butter
  • 175g Caster Sugar
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

How-to

  1. Preheat oven to 355 degrees and line two baking trays with parchment paper.
  2. Add the peanut butter and suga into a bowl with salt. Mix the Ingredients before adding the egg. Form into a dough after all ingredients are incorporated.
  3. Break off pieces of the dough and form them into smaller balls. Place them well-spaced on the tray before pressing them down with the back of a fork.
  4. Bake for 12 minutes or until edges are darker than center. Remve from oven and let cool before transferring to a container.
