Vegan Fudge Cake

A fluffy crumbly box cake made with a can of soda instead of animal products.

Ingredients

  • 1 Chocolate Box mix
  • 330ml of Flavored Soda
  • Pinch of Baking Soda

Keep in mind that the soda will lend some of its flavor to the cake. Diet soda may be used instead to lower sugar content.

How-to

  1. Preheat oven to 355 degrees. Line your cake pan with parchment.
  2. Sift cake mix into a bowl. Add in your can of soda, folding with a spoon or spatula in order to maintain air bubbles.
  3. Transfer mix into the pan and bake for 25 minutes or until skewer comes out clean from center.
  4. Let cool before decorating with chosen frosting.
