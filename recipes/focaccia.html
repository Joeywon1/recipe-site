Easy & Quick Focaccia

A soft yet crisp bread that requires little technique perfect for any beginner baker.

Ingredients

  • Bread Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Water
  • (Optional) Rosemary Sprigs
  • (Optional) Olive Oil

No exact measurements are needed, however it is best to keep to close to the suggested ratio this recipe uses. This dough is high in hydration and should be adapted based on the humidity of your enviorment.

Step by Step

  1. Gather 500 g of your chosen bread flour alongside 10 g of salt, 8 g of yeast, and 450g of water.
  2. Whisk the dry ingredients before adding the water. Add olive oil when the dough is formed if you have it.
  3. Cover the dough and stick into the fridge to rise for around 12 to 18 hours at minimum.
  4. Transfer the dough to a 9x13 pan that is greased. Leave for another 2-4 hours.
  5. Dimple your dough using your fingers. If you have any toppings add them now along with salt.
  6. In an oven set to 425 degees, place the bread into the center rack and bake for 25 minutes or until golden.
  7. Leave to rest for a few moments then slice and serve!
